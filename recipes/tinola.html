Tinola

Description

Tinola is a chicken, home hearty soup which has it's origins in Filipino cuisine.

It is, without a doubt, one of my favorite dishes. My mother usually uses chicken wings but I often forget and I go full veg.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Wings
  • Ginger
  • 3/4 Chayote
  • 1 Green Papaya
  • Loads of Bokchoy
  • Fish Sauce (to taste)
  • Water!
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Everything fits into one nice pot which is cool. Start off with the chicken wings and some olive oil (because we're being healthy). Let that brown barely with sliced ginger.
  2. Add in your water to the top.
  3. Add in the chayote and papaya.
  4. Finally, at the very end - add in your bok choy.
