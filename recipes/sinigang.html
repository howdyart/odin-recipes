Sinigang

Description

Sinigang is a sour tamarind soup which has it's origins in Filipino cuisine.

It is, without a doubt, one of my favorite dishes. My mother usually uses pork belly, but I also enjoy fish.

Ingredients:

  • Tamarind Paste (or pre-measured Sinigang Mix)
  • 1 Eggplant
  • 1 Onion
  • Handful of Okra
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • Pork Belly (or fish or none)
  • Ong Choy (a ton)
  • Fish Sauce (to taste)
  • Gabi
  • Water!
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Everything fits into one nice pot which is cool. Start off with the pork belly and some olive oil (because we're being healthy). Let that brown barely.
  2. Add in the onions and tomatoes.
  3. Add in your water to the top.
  4. Add in the okra, gabi, fish sauce, tamarind soup base, and eggplant. Let boil until soft.
  5. Finally, at the very end - add in your ong choy.
